About EatAddict

EatAddict is a food channel which aims to make cooking a cakewalk. Its about keeping the process simple but yet Innovative and Special.

This channel is a result of pure passion towards cooking and a way to inspire all those who like cooking but are sceptical about starting it. After lots of discussion and thoughts it was decided to start this channel as a medium to showcase the passion and love towards cooking and sharing kitchen secrets.

The approach is simple - Shorter and Snappier videos, detailed information of the ingredients and showcase the actual entire cooking process which helps in understanding the process a easier task.
